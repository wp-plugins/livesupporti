== Description ==

LiveSupporti plugin for WordPress allows you to add free live support chat to your WordPress website so you can chat with your visitors and provide great customer support. We offer **Forever Free Plan free of charge** with unlimited domains, unlimited concurrent chats, SSL encryption and chat history.

Get LiveSupporti Beta for **iPhone** and **iPad** <a href="https://livesupporti.com/live-chat-iphone-beta" target="_blank">here</a>

Get LiveSupporti Beta for **Android** <a href="https://livesupporti.com/live-chat-android-beta" target="_blank">here</a>

**How to install**

1. Create your free account at <a href="http://livesupporti.com/signup" target="_blank">https://livesupporti.com/signup</a>
2. Log into your WordPress admin panel, go to the **Plugins** menu, click **Add New**, search for **livesupporti** plugin and install it.
3. Click on **LiveSupporti** menu on the left and enter your product key. You're done!

== Installation ==

1. Create your free account at <a href="http://livesupporti.com/signup" target="_blank">livesupporti.com/signup</a>
2. Log into your WordPress admin panel and go to the **Plugins** menu.
3. Click **Add New**, enter **livesupporti** and click **Search Plugins**.
4. Click **Install Now**, and then **Activate Plugin**.
5. Enter your <a href="http://livesupporti.com/pk" target="_blank" title="Get product key">product key</a> and click **Save**.
